Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to connect and draw in our audience by illustrating the credibility and safety techniques of our guides, advertising the several rivers customers may want to raft on, and secure contact information, appointments, and receive payments.

Audience

The target customers are families with teenage or young adult children, and young adults and their friends. It is likely that families may use a computer or tablet and that young adults are more likely to access the site with a tablet or phone. They will expect to find a place to book appointments, a page to learn more about each rivers rafting experiences and difficulty, and to be able to get to know the company - seeing that we have experience and are trustworthy and knowledgeable in our field. They will likely expect a professional feel but with bright undertones and contrast that is easy to deal with in the dark or on dark mode. I will likely use a less saturated blue based triadic color scheme to have a nice base with softer yet vibrant accent colors.
