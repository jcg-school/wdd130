Our Purpose

Providing quality and safe adventure experiences for quality customers, their friends, and their family.

History

What initially started as a small team of passionate adventurers became something bigger than we had ever imagined. During a particularly difficult phase in her life Carolyn Von'Schweetz took a raft peacefully down Muddy Creek for a bit of inspiration. There she saw a beautiful white swan, and knowing that she wanted to follow through with providing beautiful experiences to other adventurers, decided to press on - and named her company after that experience. You can see a picture of this very swan when entering our main office.
